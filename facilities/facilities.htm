Facilities

Checkendon Pre-school has many facilities, being located next to a supportive primary school and run by experienced and dedicated staff. Tremendous opportunities exist for the children to play outside, go for walks in the woods and thrive in a stimulating atmosphere.

Facilities available

  • Healthy fruit and veg snack time

  • Outdoor play sessions

  • Superb kitchen and cooking facilities

  • Computer aided learning and play facilities

  • Nappy changing facilities

  • PE sessions

  • Integration with foundation stage children

  • Sand and water play

  • Themed arts and crafts

  • Messy play

  • Woodland walks

  • Parachute play and soft ball games

  • Music makers

  • Healthy fruit and veg snack time

  • Outdoor play sessions
