Session Times and Fees

  • Session Times
    Monday to Friday
    Mornings 8:30 to 11:30
    Lunch time 11:30 to 12:30
    Afternoon 12:30 to 3:30
    Full Day: 8.30 to 3:30

  • Session Fees
    Monday to Friday
    Mornings: £15.00 per session
    Lunch time: £5.00 per session
    Afternoon: £15.00 per session
    All Day: £35.00
    [N.B. Extra hours will be charged at £5 per hour]

As an Ofsted registered provider we offer 15 hours of Nursery Education Funding (NEF) to those children who are aged 3 years and older.

We are also a registered provider of NEF for two year olds.

We also accept childcare vouchers including Busy Bees, Fidelity, Sodexho and other providers.

Children and staff enjoy lunchtime together. Children are required to bring a packed lunch which is eaten alongside the Checkendon ladies, encouraging good manners and social interaction at mealtimes.

Children may bring a variety of foods for lunch although we do operate a NO NUTS policy due to the possibility of allergies and intolerances within the group.

During the morning and afternoon sessions a healthy snack and drink is provided, drinking water is provided at all times.
