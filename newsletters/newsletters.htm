Newsletters

     

SEPTEMBER 2007

  

Firstly a very big welcome to our new children Emily Butler, Madeleine and Alice Lee, Eilidh Venters, Beatrice Bradley, Jamie Robbins, Clara Home, Arabella Hall, George Dodd and Nadine Acharki. I also have much pleasure in welcoming Nina and Angie onto our excellent team, joining Pauline, Liz and Kelly.  We hope you’re all settling in, making new friends and enjoying your time at Pre-school.

  

The new theme this term is facial expressions, you may have seen some of the wonderful pictures on the wall already.

  

Following the Annual General Meeting recently, I can advise that we have some changes to our Committee.  Liz Allen has resigned from the role of Chairperson, during her time on the Committee Liz has been relentless in developing the Pre-school putting in a level of effort far and beyond the usual requirement of Chairperson.  So thank you Liz for all your hard work, we’re extremely grateful to you. 

  

Vicky Bauer has also resigned from the post of Treasurer, however will be staying on the Committee, which we are also very grateful for.  Sincere thanks also to Sam McDougall who has resigned from the post of Fundraiser. 

  

Our new Chairperson is now Jane Clubb, and the Treasurer, Ryan Bradley. Huge thanks to them for taking on these posts.  The next Committee meeting is on Monday 15th October and it would be great to have a few more parents involved in the Committee or just coming along to find out what’s going on and having some new ideas.

  

Now I have a favour to ask, could we please ask for raffle prizes to be donated in preparation for our Christmas Fair and the Woodcote Coffee Shop.  If we start collecting the odd bottle of wine, box of chocolates, books etc. now we should be able to hold some great raffles in November and December.  Many thanks.

  

Diary Dates:

31.10.07                                      Halloween Fun Day

14.11.07                                      Photographer at Pre-School

24.11.07                                      Woodcote Coffee Shop, 10.00am – 12.30pm

18.12.07                                      Christmas Party

21.12.07                                      End of Term

                                                                                                                       

Best wishes

Jacquie Morgan
